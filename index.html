Overview

libcbc is an ongoing project to explore the different possible APIs and abstractions for a versatile, usable, and extensible cryptographic library. The main focus is on functional encryption to support robust confidentiality in a data- and content-centric world. It is also intended to support signature schemes and symmetric-key primitives.
